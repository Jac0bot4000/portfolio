Hi, I'm Jacob Brodeur

Welcome to my portfolio website. Here, you can expect to find samples of my work in both the graphic design and web development space.

Graphic Design

Through courses such as New Media Digital Survey I and II, I have gained an understanding of and appreciation for the graphic design principles. Additionally, these classes have taught me to use the Adobe creative suite to its fullest potential, focussing specifically on Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.

I have had the opportunity to apply these skills in professional settings, from digital displays that have been run all around RIT's campus, to signage for RIT commencement, or physical print media that is permenantly installed at my home YMCA.

Web Design

At RIT, I have taken both Web & Mobile Development I and II, as well as an Introduction to Database and Data Modeling; these classes have taught me the foundations and uses of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and SQL. Using these skills, I have worked on and developed multiple websites including this portfolio site.

My focus in web development is the front end design and appearance; as such, I have served as an information architect on a semester-long group project creating a site explaining the CRAP principles. For this, I created high-fidelity wireframes and used them as a guide to style much of the content of the website. Despite my focus, I've also coded plenty of server-side functionality, such as form validation and php scripting.

Photography

Photography has always been more of a hobby of mine, where I have not recieved much professional training in the art; however, I still have a collection of landscape and object photography that I have taken and edited myself.
